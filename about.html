About

Junior Front-end developer based in the Netherlands

Hello there! I love working on my own projects in my free time and am excited to share what I have been working. Check out my work in my portfolio!

I have experience with React.js, Bootstrap, and Responsive development. I use VS Codes to write my code and all my projects are open-sourced and can by found on my GitHub profile.

I often code on the go and keep things compact with my trusty Microsoft Surface. But when I am at home I relegate the Surface to second screen duty in favor of my self-built PC (i5-13600K | RTX 4070 | 32GB DDR4 4000 CL18 | 2TB NVMe). It's a lot more comfortable to use a bigger screen (LG 27GR75Q-B) and proper mouse and keyboard (G705 | G715). (Everything decked out in RGB of course, I love that colorful lighting.)
